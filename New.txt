Task 4. Show the list of first, last names and ages of the employees whose age is greater than 55.
The result should be sorted by last name.

SELECT FirstName, LastName,CAST (DATEDIFF (Day, BirthDate, GETDATE())/365.25 AS int) AGE
FROM Employees
WHERE CAST (DATEDIFF (Day, BirthDate, GETDATE())/365.25 AS int) > 55
ORDER BY LastName

Task 18.Show the list of french customers’ names who have made more than one order (use grouping).

SELECT CompanyName, COUNT (OrderDate) AS Zakaz
FROM Customers
 INNER JOIN Orders ON Orders.CustomerID=Customers.CustomerID
 WHERE Country= 'France' 
GROUP BY CompanyName
HAVING COUNT (OrderDate) >1




Task 25.*Show the total ordering sum calculated for each country of customer.

SELECT Country, SUM (Freight) AS Suma
FROM Customers
 INNER JOIN Orders ON Orders.CustomerID=Customers.CustomerID 
GROUP BY Country
ORDER BY Suma ASC



SELECT Country, SUM (UnitPrice * Quantity) AS SUMA
FROM Customers
 INNER JOIN Orders ON Orders.CustomerID=Customers.CustomerID
 INNER JOIN [Order Details] ON Orders.OrderID=[Order Details].OrderID
GROUP BY Country
Order by SUM (UnitPrice * Quantity) ASC
